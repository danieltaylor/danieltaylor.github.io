AWS Server Migration

Although perhaps the least exciting of my writeups found on this page, I have found the documentation of processes used for my work at BYU OIT to be the most useful. While rerouting SSO traffic from old server (Elastic Beanstalk) to new (ECS/Fargate), we wanted to have as few dropped user sessions as possible. Having both servers running allowed us to avoid downtime by simply switch over the DNS to point to the new server, and copying ticket granting tickets (TGTs) from the old server's Redis store to that of the new one allowed active sessions to remain valid. The linked document describes the process required to migrate data between the two Redis stores.
